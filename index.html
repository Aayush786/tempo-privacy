Privacy Policy — Tempo

Effective Date: March 2026

Tempo is designed to help users browse with intention while respecting privacy.

Information We Collect

Tempo does not collect personally identifiable information.

All data is stored locally in your browser.

Data Usage

  • Tracked websites (user-selected)
  • Session data (intent, duration)
  • Usage history for in-extension analytics

This data is not transmitted or shared.

Permissions

  • tabs — detect active site
  • storage — save settings locally
  • activeTab — display UI overlays
  • alarms — manage timers

Your Control

You can remove all data by uninstalling the extension.
